003 / 009
THE PRODUCT
How it works

OctOpus replaces obsolete AutoML.
It powers data scientists and lets non-technical teams ship reliable models too.

  • 01 → You upload your data and ask a question. Spreadsheet, database, or file. Customer churn, customer-level forecasts, revenue, demand, defects.
  • 02 → OctOpus plans the work an ML team normally has to do. It inspects the data, frames the task, chooses features, selects models, and prepares the path to deployment.
  • 03 → It tries, debugs, and improves until the model is reliable. Not just code generation: it runs experiments, fixes failures, compares approaches, and keeps going until the result is good enough to trust.
  • 04 → You get a deployed model and something the business can use. A reliable model, a report, and output your team can actually act on — whether the user is a data scientist, analyst, or operator.
